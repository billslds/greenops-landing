The compliance crisis

Every application is a legal obligation.
Are you protected?

As a licensed commercial pesticide applicator, every application you make must be documented — the product, EPA registration number, amount applied, weather conditions, applicator license, and more. These aren't suggestions. They're state law.

Most lawn care software was built for mowing routes and scheduling. None of them understand pesticide compliance. That leaves you exposed — using spreadsheets, paper logs, or nothing at all — hoping you never get audited.

GreenOps was built from scratch by someone who spent 35 years in the field facing exactly this problem. Every feature exists because a real applicator needed it.

⚠
State fines for missing records
State regulators audit commercial applicators. Missing or incomplete application records can result in license suspension and fines up to $5,000 per violation.
📋
48-hour notification requirements
Sensitive site notifications, school notifications, and customer alerts — each with specific timing requirements. Manual tracking leaves you vulnerable.
✓
GreenOps automates all of it
Application records generate automatically when you complete a work order. Reports are always current, always accurate, always ready for inspection.
